# El Presidente

- Your role is to be all-knowing (generally) with everything KTP. You must be a natural leader and a role model to those outside of KTP. Be kind, just, and **always vote last on decisions**. Why? The Executive board should generally be an odd amount of members, if voting is tied you should always be the tie breaker. You must be quick on your feet, methodical in decision making, kind, and just.
- The better you delegate tasks to your Eboad, Cabinet, Nationals, and General Body; the less work will be had for you (this is a good thing).

---

## Constitution

You must uphold the values of our constitution, this includes (but is not limited to) our code of conduct and behavior outside of the University of Georiga. The constitution acts as a framework that the founders and previous Eboard members have created to ensure an amazing experience.

---

## Work

While delegation is important, you must be present at many rush/PNM events. This includes all Info Sessions (If possible). Your voice, position, and comments are **the most** influential in the whole fraternity. Use this power wisely. Also, you are the main speaker for all: Chapter Meetings, Executive Board Meetings, and Special Meetings (see constitution).
