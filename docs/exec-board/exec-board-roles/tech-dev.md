# VP of Technical Development

- The **primairy** role of the VP of Technical Development is to elevate the technical skills and knowledge of the fraternity in the pursuit of **making our brothers more competitve** for landing internships and jobs at top companies.
- The **secondary** role of the VP of Technical Development is to cultivate a community that **encourages intellectutal curiosity and exploration** in the fields of computer science, data science, software engineering, and related fields.
- The VP of Technical Development has the freedom to pursue these goals creatively, typically by **leading engaging workshops** or technical intiaitves that are useful, relevant, interesting, and/ or novel.
